# 📊 YouTube Content Strategy Analysis

🚀 A data-driven analysis of YouTube trends to uncover insights on content performance, engagement, and optimal publishing strategies.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes trending YouTube video data to identify patterns in **views, engagement, content categories, and publishing strategies**.  
It provides actionable insights for **content creators and advertisers** to maximize reach and engagement.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify key drivers of views and engagement  
- Determine optimal posting time and upload day

## 📊 Key Analysis & Insights

### 📌 Content Strategy Insights

- How-to, Tech, and Education categories show **highest engagement rates**  
- Entertainment has **high reach but lower engagement quality**  
- Niche categories build **more loyal audiences**  

---

### ⏰ Best Time to Upload

- Peak performance: **9–10 AM**  
- Secondary peak: **6–8 PM**  
- Low performance: midday & late night  

---

### 📅 Best Day to Upload

- **Friday** generates highest views  
- Weekdays outperform weekends  
- **Saturday performs the worst**

---

### 📈 Engagement & Correlation

- Strong correlation between **likes & comments (0.82)**  
- Views strongly correlate with likes (0.79)  
- Engagement rate has weaker direct correlation with views  

---

### 👑 Creator & Market Insights

- Engagement is dominated by **top creators (high concentration)**  
- Education creators have **high engagement despite smaller scale**  
- Lifestyle & fashion creators maintain consistent interaction  

---

### 📊 Category Insights

- **Music dominates total views**  
- Entertainment is second highest  
- Gaming & niche categories have lower reach
